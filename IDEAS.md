Idea Planner Expansion

Private Ideation Rooms – secure invitation-only rooms with role-based controls where admins trigger timed ideation phases and participants see synchronized countdowns.
Timed Brainstorm Sprint – 30-second rapid-fire input mode with auto-summarized bullet bubbles per participant plus optional AI hints seeded by admin prompts.
Voting & Shortlisting – 5-minute ranked-choice or dot-voting session with live tally, tie-break suggestions, and AI clustering to merge near-duplicate ideas.
Highlight & Prompt – automatic callout of the winning idea, breakout side panels for “interesting sub-ideas,” and context-aware prompts nudging deeper exploration.
Infinite Sub-Detail Tree – hierarchical idea node editor supporting nested sub-points, attachments, and references; collapsible tree view with quick-jump breadcrumbs.
Flowchaty Visualization – dynamic flowchart that maps ideation → voting → elaboration → decisions, with draggable nodes and annotations showing ownership and timestamps.
LLM-powered Kanban – convert selected ideas into Kanban cards with autogenerated descriptions, acceptance criteria, risk tags, and auto-suggested task breakdowns.
DevOps Decision Trees – AI-generated branching plans that propose architecture, tooling, CI/CD paths, and testing strategies, editable by admins.
Summarization Hub – final recap combining main idea, key sub-ideas, vote statistics, action items, and suggested next steps; exportable as PDF/Markdown.
